# CODEX Data Review

Scope: source-to-claim audit for `data/generated/sources.json`, `lib/source-manifest.ts`, `lib/buyer-report.ts`, `scripts/normalize.ts`, and `scripts/score.ts`. Verdicts are about citation support and reproducibility, not a full row-by-row recomputation of every SA2 value.

| Claim | Cited source | Verdict (supported / weak / unsupported) | Evidence or URL | Recommended fix |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Affordability score uses SA2 rent-to-income ratio, computed as 2021 median weekly rent divided by 2021 median equivalised household income. | `abs-rent-to-income-2021`, derived from `abs-census-rent-2021` and `abs-sa2-income-dbr`. | supported | ABS Data by Region exposes Census median weekly rent and median equivalised total household income fields at SA2 level: https://www.abs.gov.au/databyregion and https://dbr.abs.gov.au/. The manifest states this is a derived ratio. | Keep the source chain, but keep wording conservative: this is a ratio of two SA2 medians, not measured household rent stress or buyer affordability. |
| Median weekly rent figure. | `abs-census-rent-2021`. | supported | ABS Data by Region / Census 2021 supports median weekly household rental payment at SA2 level: https://www.abs.gov.au/databyregion. | Keep 2021 period visible. Do not imply this is current market rent. |
| Median equivalised household income figure. | `abs-sa2-income-dbr`. | supported | ABS Data by Region exposes "Median equivalised total household income (weekly)" for Census 2021: https://dbr.abs.gov.au/. Local manifest has hash `6d90892da6b33d8bec301643422e7c01bd4ffe33586f087a2b314a6d030939f3`. | Keep 2021 period visible. Do not present as current income. |
| Employment ratio and labour participation are scored as an income/employment domain. | `abs-census-labour-2016`. | weak | The source is real ABS 2016 Data by Region, but it is stale relative to the app's 2021 Census sources and the score still uses it. `scripts/score.ts` marks old sources stale, but the score still includes the indicator. | Replace with 2021 labour-force fields if available, or remove/downweight these indicators until refreshed. Label any remaining use as 2016 Census, not current employment conditions. |
| Population totals, growth, and density context use ERP SA2 data. | `abs-erp-sa2`, `abs-erp-sa2-series`. | weak | Manifest periods are `2023` and `2001-2023`. ABS latest Regional Population is now 2024-25, released 31 March 2026, with SA2 ERP at 30 June 2025: https://www.abs.gov.au/statistics/people/population/regional-population/latest-release. Density also needs a cited land-area or boundary source, not ERP alone. | Refresh ERP to the latest ABS release, update periods and hashes, and cite the SA2 boundary/area source used for density. |
| Transport score uses stops within 800 m, scheduled AM peak frequency, and number of public transport modes. | `ptv-gtfs` or `osm-pt` fallback. | supported | Transport Victoria GTFS Schedule is a weekly/static timetable dataset for Victoria and includes train, tram, bus and coach modes: https://opendata.transport.vic.gov.au/dataset/gtfs-schedule. | Keep language as "scheduled" and "static timetable". Do not imply real-time service, cancellations, crowding, or reliability. Surface when OSM fallback was used instead of PTV GTFS. |
| Safety score uses property-crime and violent-crime rates mapped to suburbs/LGAs. | `vcsa-recorded-offences`. | weak | VCSA / DataVic supports recorded offences by offence type and geography, but the manifest period `2025 (latest year in export)` is imprecise. Local workbook contents show October 2015 to September 2025, while DataVic now lists "Recorded offences - Year ending Dec 2025" released March 2026: https://discover.data.vic.gov.au/dataset/data-tables-recorded-offences. The manifest URL also returns 403 in local source verification. | Change period to "year ending September 2025" for the current local file, use the stable DataVic dataset/resource URL, and refresh to year-ending December 2025 if the app is meant to be current. Keep the suburb/LGA-not-street caveat. |
| Distance to nearest public hospital. | `vic-mapshare-hospitals` or OSM fallback via `osm-health`. | weak | The cited Vicmap source is an Emergency Services / Features of Interest point layer, and the manifest name says "general hospitals"; that does not by itself prove "public hospital". URL: https://enterprise.mapshare.vic.gov.au/server/rest/services/Hosted/Emergency_Services__VMFEAT_FOI_POINT_/FeatureServer. | Rename the metric to "nearest mapped general hospital" unless the extraction explicitly filters to public hospitals and that filter is documented. If OSM fallback is used, show that caveat. |
| GP/clinic count within 2 km. | `osm-health`. | supported | Manifest and `scripts/normalize.ts` use an OSM Overpass extract for GP/clinic points. | Schools within 2 km and childcare/preschool access context. | `osm-schools`, `abs-census-preschool-2021`, and sometimes `vicmap-foi`. | supported | OSM supports mapped school/childcare POI proximity: https://www.openstreetmap.org/. Vicmap FOI can support official feature points: https://services-ap1.arcgis.com/P744lA0wf4LlBZ84/ArcGIS/rest/services/Vicmap_Features_of_Interest/FeatureServer. | Keep separate from catchment/zoning claims. | School-zone advice says official zones are not included and points users to Find My School. | Inline `vic-school-zones` source, not in manifest. | supported | The limitation is reasonable and the official service exists: https://www.findmyschool.vic.gov.au/. | Add this limitation source to the manifest, or clearly separate non-data limitation references from manifest-backed data sources. |
| Bushfire hazard share is an SA2 area share of designated Bushfire Prone Area. | `vic-planning-bpa`. | supported | Vicmap Planning BPA layer ID 9 is an official polygon source: https://services-ap1.arcgis.com/P744lA0wf4LlBZ84/ArcGIS/rest/services/Vicmap_Planning/FeatureServer/9. | Flood hazard share is an SA2 area share of flood planning overlays. | `vic-planning-flood`. | weak | Manifest name says LSIO only and notes SBO unavailable, with hash for `vic-lsio.geojson`. `scripts/normalize.ts` can combine LSIO and SBO if both files exist, but local raw data lacks `vic-sbo.geojson`. | Heritage overlay share. | `vic-planning-heritage`. | supported | Vicplan Planning Scheme Overlays includes Heritage Overlay layer 9: https://plan-gis.mapshare.vic.gov.au/arcgis/rest/services/Planning/Vicplan_PlanningSchemeOverlays/MapServer.
